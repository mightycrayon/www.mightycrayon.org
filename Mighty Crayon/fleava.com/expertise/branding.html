/ Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is Brand Strategy?

     +

    By definition, a brand strategy is a long-term plan for the development of a brand, with the objective of implying a particular image of itself in the minds of its audience. In other words, strategies on how you want people to perceive your Brand.

    Read: The Essence of Branding

  2. Why do I need a strategy for my Brand?

     +

    Your company requires a strategy if you want to build a strong brand. You can develop a powerful brand with the support of an effective brand strategy.

    It defines your company's identity, the brand personality, and focuses on your long-term goals. You may maximize your return on investment if your brand is strong and your brand strategy is successful.

    It is no secret that consumers prefer to identify with well-known and prestigious brands. Because of this, business owners must work to build solid brands. This is where brand strategy comes in.

    Read: Your Guide to Branding and Why it Matters

  3. What is Brand Positioning and why is it important?

     +

    Brand positioning explains how a brand differentiates from its rivals and how it is perceived by consumers. In order to influence how people see a brand, a brand positioning strategy entails building brand connections in their thoughts.

    Positioning strategies have a direct impact on consumer loyalty, consumer-based brand equity, and brand readiness to buy by influencing consumer choices.

    Effective brand positioning can be defined as the degree to which a brand is regarded favorably, distinctively, and credibly by customers.

  4. Why do I need a Brand Guideline?

     +

    Brand guidelines is a crucial tool, regardless of the size of your business, to assist maintain brand consistency. Without rules, brands can change over time and potentially harm your reputation and brand value.

  5. How do you measure a successful Brand?

     +

    Branding success — or failure — is usually measured across a much larger arch of time. You have to figure out what to measure.

    Since branding can mean different things to different people, you have to decide which metrics you wish to increase. Branding can increase sales, increase online chatter, increase mention in media, and website traffic.

    Read: Branding: Be the Subconscious of your Customer

  6. Do you do standalone services like just a Logo design?

     +

    Yes, we do standalone services such as Logo design, Brand naming, brand research etc. You don't have to take the full-service — However, for a more successful result we strongly recommend to do the complete aspects of our Branding services.

  7. How much do your Branding services cost?

     +

    Our price for branding services is varied, depending on the size of the project, the brand structure & architecture, market size, etc. Contact us or fill out our inquiry forms to get a quotation for our service.
