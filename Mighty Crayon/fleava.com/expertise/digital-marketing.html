/ Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Why do I need Digital Campaign for my Brand?

    +

    Digital campaign is crucial because it links a business with its customers once they are online and works in all industries. It enables you to demonstrate to clients what your company is built of and how you can meet their needs.

    Digital marketing generates more revenue than traditional marketing since it offers a higher return on investment and conversion rate.

    Read: The Ultimate Guide to Digital Marketing

  2. What is SEO?

    +

    The term "search engine optimization" (SEO) refers to the practice of obtaining visitors for unpaid, organic, or natural search results in browsers. It seeks to raise your website's position in search engine results. The higher it is ranked the more people will visit a website.

  3. What's the most effective SEO Strategy?

    +

    SEO is a collective of efforts, and really everything matters. Generally however, the top three most effective SEO strategies are Website optimization, regular quality content posting, and link building.

    Read: UX is the new SEO; The Power of Blogging

  4. How long can I see the result of my SEO?

    +

    SEO takes time to perform, in contrast to other well-liked marketing channels. Many SEO agencies will tell you that it takes 4 to 6 months to start seeing improvements. That's usually accurate.

    Although every company has a unique SEO approach, the majority of companies may anticipate significant benefits in 4 to 6 months, but in some cases, it can be up to 12 months.

  5. Why does my business need a Social Media?

    +

    Social media can assist your business in attracting customers, receiving customer feedback, cultivating customer loyalty, and expand your market reach to include even global markets.

    The answer is rather simple — everyone now is on Social Media, and from there you can reach any market in any segment and demographic.

    Read: The Exponential Impact of Social Media & SEO

  6. Which Social Media Platforms are the most effective?

    +

    The most effective Social Media Platforms based on the top five most popular social media in the world are Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook Messenger.
