/ Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What does the video production process involve?

    +

    Each project is different, but the production process can be broadly divided into three phases.

    Pre-Production is where all the preparations going on, from concepting, location scouting, talent casting, etc. Production phase is when the shooting take time and place. Post Production is once we've captured all the footage, and start editing and stitch the footage together into a cohesive story, adding graphics, titles, and music as needed.

  2. How large is your crew for filming?

    +

    It depends on the type of work. For a simple project, we go with a crew of four to six, consisting of a producer & director who oversees the shoot, a DOP & Camera Operator who focus on the visual and technical aspects, and lighting assistants.

    For more complex shooting requirements, we can go with bigger team, which sometimes can go up to tens to hundreds of people for bigger production projects.

  3. Do you do Sound Design and Scoring?

    +

    Yes we do Sound production services. We can source a track from a royalty-free music library and provide a license for usage in the finished video, or for projects that require a tailor-made music & sound, we do custom sound design and music scoring as well.

  4. What kind of equipment do you use?

    +

    We shoot everything with a professional broadcast quality 4K video cameras and use additional equipment such as wireless microphones, lighting kits and backdrops as needed. We continue to invest in new equipment and software to meet rapidly changing industry standards.

  5. How much do your Media Production services cost?

    +

    Our price for media production services is varied, depending on the size of the project, how many shooting days, locations, etc. Contact us or fill out our inquiry forms to get a quotation for our service.
