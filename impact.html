Success Stories

Real stories of impact from our crayon recycling initiative.

Oakwood Elementary School

"The crayons donated by Mighty Crayon have inspired our students to spend more time drawing and less time on screens. We've seen a notable improvement in fine motor skills and creative expression."

- Sarah Johnson, Art Teacher

Hopetown Children's Home, Kenya

"Many of our children had never had their own art supplies before. The joy on their faces when they received the crayons was indescribable. They now spend hours creating colorful artwork and expressing themselves."

- Daniel Omondi, Director

Children's Hospital Creative Therapy

"The crayons donated by Mighty Crayon have been instrumental in our art therapy programs. They help our young patients express their emotions, process their experiences, and find moments of joy during difficult times."

- Dr. Rebecca Chen, Child Psychologist
